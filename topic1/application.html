About IoT Applications

IoT (Internet of Things) applications refer to the use of connected devices to collect and transmit data to other devices or systems over the internet. These applications can be found in various industries, from healthcare to manufacturing, and are used to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance the customer experience.
